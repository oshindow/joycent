Diffusion-based Front-End-Free Accent Text-to-Speech with Phone-Level Style Tokens

Abstract

This work proposes a diffusion-based accent TTS model that removes the reliance on front-end phoneme encoders. The system introduces phone-level style tokens to capture accent variations while preserving speaker identity. Experiments demonstrate robust performance on both seen and unseen speakers, as well as across multiple accents.
